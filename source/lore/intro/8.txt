Through discoveries found by the Manila Institute of Technomancy in the fields of pharmagicology and medical technomancy, treatments for highly severe physical injuries (i.e., blood loss, gunshot wounds, torn organs, and even death) are now available for the extremely affluent members of society and most soldiers of the Armed Forces of the Philippines.

In the case of T3D combatants/champions, their post-battle treatments are usually sponsored by their parent corporations. However, in the case of unsponsored champions, their treatments are paid for by the Tondo Dome Association. If they are unable to obtain a sponsor after their first two years of fighting, they are forcibly retired from fighting under the TDA
